## 示例 2：图灵机 — 六维内容补充

**文档标题（中英对照）**

图灵机 — 六维内容补充 / Turing Machine — Six-Dimensional Supplement
`docs/07-计算模型/01-图灵机-六维补充.md`

**摘要正文 / Abstract**

This graduate-level supplement provides a six-dimensional elaboration of the Turing machine (TM): concept definition, properties, relations, explanation, argumentation, and formal proof. It gives the complete formal definition of deterministic and nondeterministic TMs, multi-tape and universal variants, and instantaneously describes configurations. The document then develops the classical theory of decidability and computability, presents the undecidability of the Halting Problem, and introduces mapping reductions (≤m) and Turing reductions (≤T) as essential tools for hardness proofs. Aligned with MIT 18.404, CMU 15-251, and UCSD CSE 105, it bridges intuitive understanding with rigorous derivations.

**关键词 / Keywords**

- 图灵机 (Turing Machine)
- 可判定性 (Decidability)
- 停机问题 (Halting Problem)
- 丘奇-图灵论题 (Church-Turing Thesis)

## 示例 3：证明系统 — 六维内容补充

**文档标题（中英对照）**

证明系统 — 六维内容补充 / Proof System — Six-Dimensional Supplement
`docs/03-形式化证明/01-证明系统-六维补充.md`

**摘要正文 / Abstract**

This document enriches the theory of proof systems through six cognitive dimensions: definition, attributes, relations, intuition, argumentation, and formal proof. A proof system is formally introduced as a triple (ℒ, 𝒜, ℛ) comprising a language, axioms, and inference rules. The text systematically covers Natural Deduction with introduction/elimination rule pairs, Hilbert-style axiomatic systems with Modus Ponens, and Sequent Calculus including the Cut rule and its eliminability. Extensions to propositional, first-order, and higher-order logic are discussed, complete with mind maps and dependency graphs that clarify the structural relationships among these frameworks.

**关键词 / Keywords**

- 证明系统 (Proof System)
- 自然演绎 (Natural Deduction)
- 序列演算 (Sequent Calculus)
- 切割消除 (Cut Elimination)
- 希尔伯特系统 (Hilbert System)

## 示例 4：证明系统

**文档标题（中英对照）**

3.1 证明系统 / Proof System
`docs/03-形式化证明/01-证明系统.md`

**摘要正文 / Abstract**

This module unifies three major proof frameworks—axiomatic systems, Natural Deduction, and Sequent Calculus—under a single proof-complexity perspective. It elaborates soundness and completeness (Γ ⊢ φ ⇔ Γ ⊨ φ), the Curry–Howard correspondence connecting proofs to programs, and practical applications in type theory and formal verification. The document also surveys contemporary developments, including AI-assisted proof generation, verification competitions such as SV-COMP, and the educational adoption of lightweight “FM thinking.” Cross-references to dependent types, Hoare logic, and model checking ensure seamless navigation across the curriculum.

**关键词 / Keywords**

- 公理系统 (Axiomatic System)
- 可靠性 (Soundness)
- 完备性 (Completeness)
- 证明复杂性 (Proof Complexity)
- Curry-Howard对应 (Curry–Howard Correspondence)

## 示例 5：算法设计理论

**文档标题（中英对照）**

9.1.1 算法设计理论 / Algorithm Design Theory
`docs/09-算法理论/01-算法基础/01-算法设计理论.md`

**摘要正文 / Abstract**

This foundational text surveys the core paradigms of algorithm design—divide-and-conquer, dynamic programming, and greedy algorithms—while integrating correctness proofs and asymptotic complexity analysis. The document serves as a navigational hub, cross-referencing both elementary algorithm basics and advanced topics in optimization, randomized algorithms, and online algorithms.
